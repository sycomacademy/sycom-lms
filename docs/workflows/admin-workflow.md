# Admin Workflow

This guide walks through the platform administrator experience on Sycom LMS, from account creation to managing users and viewing platform analytics.

---

## 1. Create Your Account

1. Navigate to the **Sign Up** page (`/sign-up`).
2. Fill in your details:
   - **First name** and **Last name**
   - **Email address** (use your organization email)
   - **Password** (minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, and a number)
3. Click **Create account**.
4. You will be redirected to the Sign In page with a confirmation message.

## 2. Verify Your Email

After signing up, check your inbox for a verification email from Sycom LMS. Click the verification link to activate your account.

> **Note:** Your account must be verified before you can sign in.

## 3. Get Admin Access

A platform administrator must assign you the **Platform Admin** role. Contact your organization's existing admin to update your role. Once assigned, you will have full access to the admin dashboard.

## 4. Sign In

1. Navigate to the **Sign In** page (`/sign-in`).
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click **Continue**.
4. You will be redirected to the **Admin Dashboard**.

## 5. Admin Dashboard Overview

After signing in, the admin dashboard displays:

- **Platform statistics** at a glance:
  - Total **Users** across all roles
  - **Organizations** currently active
  - **Active invites** (open creator and admin invites)
  - **Pending reports** waiting for triage
- **User growth chart** showing account creation trends over the past 7 days
- **Latest signups** with user names, emails, roles, and join dates
- **Recent inbox** for reports and feedback

## 6. Managing Users

From the sidebar, click **Users** to access the user management page. Here you can:

- **Search** users by name or email
- **Filter** by role (Platform Admin, Content Creator, Student) and status (Active, Banned)
- View a table of all users with:
  - Name and avatar
  - Email address
  - Role badge
  - Organization membership
  - Account status
  - Join date
- **Invite new users** using the **+ New Invite** button
- Access user actions via the **...** menu on each row

## 7. Additional Admin Pages

The admin sidebar also provides access to:

| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Public Invites** | Manage invitation links for onboarding new users |
| **Enrollments** | View and manage course enrollments across the platform |
| **Organizations** | Create and manage organizations and their members |
| **Reports & Feedback** | Review reports and feedback submitted by users |

## 8. Navigation

The admin sidebar is organized into two sections:

- **Main**: Overview, Users
- **Courses**: All Courses, All Posts
- **General**: Support, Settings
