# Student Workflow

This guide walks through the student experience on Sycom LMS, from account creation to browsing courses, enrolling, completing lessons, and answering quizzes.

<video src="videos/student_workflow_demo.mp4" controls width="100%"></video>

---

## 1. Create Your Account

1. Navigate to the **Sign Up** page (`/sign-up`).
2. Fill in your details:
   - **First name** and **Last name**
   - **Email address**
   - **Password** (minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, and a number)
3. Click **Create account**.
4. You will be redirected to the Sign In page with a confirmation message.

## 2. Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email from Sycom LMS. Click the verification link to activate your account.

> **Note:** Your account must be verified before you can sign in. New accounts are assigned the **Student** role by default.

## 3. Sign In

1. Navigate to the **Sign In** page (`/sign-in`).
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click **Continue**.
4. You will be redirected to the **Student Dashboard**.

## 4. Student Dashboard Overview

The student dashboard shows your learning progress at a glance:

- **Quick statistics**:
  - Courses **In progress**
  - Courses **Completed**
  - **Lessons cleared**
  - **Overall progress** percentage
- **Continue learning** — pick up the next lesson in your most active course
- **Recent course activity** — your latest enrollments and completion status
- **Discover something new** — recently updated courses you have not enrolled in yet

## 5. Browsing the Library

Click **Library** in the sidebar to browse all published courses. The library page lets you:

- **Search** for courses by name
- **Filter** by category and difficulty level
- **Sort** by recently updated
- View course cards showing:
  - Thumbnail image
  - Title and difficulty badge
  - Category
  - Description
  - Number of lessons and enrolled students

## 6. Viewing Course Details

Click **View course** on any course card to see the full course page:

- Course **title**, **difficulty badge**, and **description**
- **Course summary** — a detailed overview of topics and outcomes
- **Course curriculum** — list of all sections and lessons
- Enrollment panel showing lesson count and the **Enroll in course** button

## 7. Enrolling in a Course

1. On the course detail page, click **Enroll in course**.
2. A confirmation toast ("Enrolled successfully") appears.
3. The button changes to **Continue Learning**.
4. Click **Continue Learning** to start the course.

## 8. Learning Interface

After enrolling and starting a course, the learning interface displays:

- **Left sidebar**: Course title, progress bar, and a navigable list of all sections and lessons (completed lessons show a checkmark)
- **Main content area**: The current lesson content (text, images, videos, quizzes)
- **Bottom bar**:
  - **Previous** / **Next** navigation buttons
  - **Mark complete** button (bottom-right)

## 9. Completing Lessons

To progress through the course:

1. Read or watch the lesson content.
2. Click **Mark complete** at the bottom-right to mark the current lesson as done.
3. The lesson title in the sidebar gains a **checkmark** icon.
4. Click **Next** to proceed to the following lesson.
5. Your **Progress** percentage updates automatically in the sidebar.

## 10. Answering Quizzes

Some lessons include interactive **Quiz** blocks (typically after video content). To answer a quiz:

1. Scroll down past the lesson content to find the **Quiz** section.
2. Read the question carefully.
3. Click on the answer option you believe is correct.
4. Click **Check Answer** to submit.
5. The result appears immediately:
   - **Correct**: The selected option turns green with a checkmark
   - **Incorrect**: You can click **Try Again** to attempt the question again
6. Continue to the next lesson or mark the current one complete.

### Example Quiz

After watching the OSI Model video, you might encounter:

- **Question**: "What are the 7 layers of the OSI model from bottom to top?"
- Select: **Physical, Data Link, Network, Transport, Session, Presentation, Application**
- Click **Check Answer** to verify

## 11. Tracking Your Progress

- The **sidebar progress bar** shows your completion percentage for the current course
- The **dashboard overview** aggregates progress across all enrolled courses
- Visit **My Courses** in the sidebar to see all your active enrollments and jump back into any course
